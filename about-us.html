About us

Aberdeen Protects is the overarching name being given to cover a range of partnership structures within the city. These partnership structures are governed by the Chief Officers Group who are represented by each agency and provide the leadership and support that these partnership structures require.

Agencies within these structures are made up of organisations that work together on a multi agency basis to safeguard and promote the welfare of adults, children, young people and their families across Aberdeen City.
